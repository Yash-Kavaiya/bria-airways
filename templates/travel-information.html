London Heathrow - Our home hub

British Airways operates from Terminals 3 and 5 at London Heathrow. Terminal 5 is our state-of-the-art home with world-class shopping, dining and lounge facilities.

  • Fast-track security for eligible customers
  • Multiple British Airways lounges
  • Premium check-in zones
  • Direct Heathrow Express train from London
